Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for Lasagna. It is layers of cheese, meat, flat noodles and whatever else you may want


Ingredients

  • Flat Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil flat noodles first so that when your are ready to assemble they will be done
  2. Cook ground beef until the outside is no longer pink
  3. Pre heat oven to 350 F
  4. Begin to assemble the Lasagna in a bake safe pan. The steps for assembly should be flat noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce and cheese. Repeat these steps until you have reached the top of the pan
