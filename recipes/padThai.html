Pad Thai

Description

This is a recipe for some mucho bueno pad thai!


Ingredients

  • Rice Noodle
  • Pad Thai Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Peanuts
  • Chicken
  • Tofu

Cooking Steps

  1. Prep rice noodles by following the instructions on the bag
  2. Precook chicken and slice up tofu into cubes
  3. Throw chicken and pad thai sauce into a pan and allow it to warm up
  4. Add peanuts and sliced green onions
  5. Serve!
