Carne Asada

Description

This is a recipe to make a scrumptious carne asada!


Ingredients

  • Skirt Steak
  • Oranges
  • White Onion
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chunky Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Grab a gallon ziploc bag and throw the skirt steak into it.
  2. Juice three oranges and pour the juice into the bag.
  3. Chop onion into half moon slices and throw into the bag.
  4. Add garlic powder and pepper into the bag then let out the air in the bag and seal it. Allow the skirt steak to marinate for one hour.
  5. Take out skirt steak and begin to preheat grill
  6. Put skirt steak on the grill and salt both sides with chunky sea salt
  7. Cook till medium rare then once it is done let it rest for five minutes then serve!
